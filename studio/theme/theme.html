CORE STUDIO INTERACTION THEME

MY THEME FOR THE SEMESTERS WILL BE VISUAL DESIGN FOR MUSIC,
FOCUSING MAINLY ON THE DESIGN OF ALBUM COVERS.
HOWEVER, I WILL ALSO EXPLORE OTHER VISUAL ELEMENTS SUCH AS
THE VISUAL ALBUM, USE OF TYPOGRAPHY IN MUSIC VIDEOS,
AND ALBUM ART RELEASED IN CONJUCTION WITH ALBUM RELEASES.
