TALK

Show Me The Body is a New York based hardcore punk band. Their debut album Body War was released in 2016. While they are a punk band, their music draws heavily from hip hop, noise, and metal as well. The band strives to celebrate New York City, and New York City youth. Their sound is chaotic and energetic. gritty and direct. They have recently launched a new video series called 'Corpus tv' which is available on their website. The video series features collaborations amongst different artists in New York, celebrating New York's youth culture.

Most recently, Show Me the Body revealed a new music video for their song "Talk". The song is about remaining free within the plague of masculinity and capitalism. The video features dancers dancing in circles along to the music, in various locations from an underpass, to what looks like a forrest of some sort, to an undisclosed venue.

The art released with "Talk" features a black and white emblem of sorts. On the outside of the circle in handwritten text are the words "Show me the body","Corpus", and "Talk". There are also little hand drawn stars on the outside of the circle. The inside features an illustration of a figure with a mohawk pointing to a chalk board in front of a large group of people that reads "we are so punk. we are so core."
