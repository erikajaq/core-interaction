DANCEDANCEDANCE

DANCE DANCE DANCE IS ENOCH HENRY LIGHT'S JAZZ ROCK ALBUM FROM 1964. THE ALBUM WAS RELEASED IN 1964 IN CANADA. THE ALBUM HAS 13 TRACKS ON IT SUCH AS "DREAM LOVER", "YA YA", AND "I WANT TO BE HAPPY".

ENOCH'S ALBUM COVERS ARE TYPOGRAPHICAL TREATS. THEY OFTEN FEATURE BOLD DISPLAY TYPE AND SIMPLE, ALMOST ABSTRACT SHAPES REPRESENTATIONAL SHAPES.
