SOME GIRLS

Some Girls by the Rolling Stones was released in June 1978. It was a chart topper in both the US and the UK. It quickly became the band's best selling album in the US and has sold over six million copies as of 2000, as well as the only Rolling Stones album to be nominated for a Grammy.
The album cover was designed by Peter Corriston and features illustrations by Hubert Kretzschman. The cover was designed with various colors and cuts on the sleeves for different markets. It includes the faces of some of the band members as well as female celebrities.
Peter Corriston is a Grammy-award winning graphic designer. He is well known for designing album artwork for rock bands. He's worked with the Rolling Stones, Carole King, Led Zeppelin, Billy Idol, Rod Stewart, etc. Hubert Kretzschman is also a well known artist who has collaborated numberous times with various artists, mainly in the field of Rock music.
